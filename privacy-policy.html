Privacy Policy

NautiKit — Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

NautiKit is a nautical reference and calculator app developed by Miguel Figuerola. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

NautiKit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works entirely offline on your device.

What stays on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server:

  • Checklist profiles and items you create
  • Your app preferences (selected units, categories)

This data is managed by Apple's on-device frameworks (SwiftData and UserDefaults) and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

NautiKit does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. No usage data is collected.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
